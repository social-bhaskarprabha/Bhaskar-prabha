About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Tejas Kulkarni Aacharya and Aditya Maheshwari Sir, Bhaskarprabha Foundation is India’s first traditional Ganit Shastra Paathshala (Maths Gurukul) dedicated to core mathematics education.

Tejas Kulkarni Aacharya completed the Mahapariksha in Bharatiya Ganitshastra at Shringeri Peeth, and Aditya Maheshwari Sir is a Professor at IIM Indore.

We blend ancient wisdom—drawing from timeless granthas—with modern mathematical approaches to create a learning experience that connects the past with contemporary applications.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: Bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and modern applications.
  • Mission: Empower students with deep, personalized learning in both ancient and modern mathematics, transforming them into confident, independent problem solvers.

Our Principles (The Gurukul Ethos)

Free Education & Facilities

Education, accommodation, and all related activities are provided free of charge.

Swavalamban

Students take responsibility for daily routines, developing discipline, independence, and leadership.

Adhyayan & Adhyapan

Learning by teaching strengthens understanding and boosts confidence.

Cultural Integrity

All instruction and communication take place in Sanskrit, preserving tradition.

Our Programs

1. Ganit Shastra (Ancient Mathematics)

Our flagship 5-year course is designed for students aspiring to become pandits in Ganit Shastra.

Curriculum Overview:

The course includes study of 10 Granthas, divided between ancient texts in Sanskrit and modern subjects.

Ancient Granthas:
  • Leelavati – Foundational text
  • Beejganitam – Groundwork for deeper understanding
  • Saraltrikonmiti
  • Rekhaganitam
  • Pratibhabodhakam
  • Ganitsaarsangraha etc.
Modern Granthas:
  • Algebra for Beginners
  • Coordinate Geometry
  • Number Theory
  • Calculus etc.

Examinations:

Indian Mathematics Exams (Ganit Shastra) are conducted twice a year (June and December) at Shree Datta Devasthan Trust, Ahilyanagar (formerly Ahmednagar), Maharashtra.

2. Modern Mathematics (Online)

Learn math easily from home with our online classes that cover:

  • Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, and more
  • Live sessions, recorded lectures, and personalized mentoring
  • An approach that simplifies complex topics and builds lasting confidence

3. Statistics and Computer Science

This program integrates the rigor of statistical methods with computer science fundamentals, preparing students for data analysis, computational problem-solving, and modern mathematical applications.

Residential Program

Experience Gurukul Living:

Immerse yourself fully in a traditional learning environment on campus. Live alongside peers, attend daily lessons, and build strong mentor relationships.

Day Program

Flexibility for Local Students:

Attend classes during the day without residential commitments, enjoying the same high-quality education while living at home.

Current Activities

  • New students start their journey with the Leelavati Granth.
  • Progressive learning through Bhartiya Ganit Shastra Granthas (Bijganit, Trikonmiti, Rekha Ganit, etc.).
  • Introduction to Nyaya Shastra using Vidyadhari by M. M. Devdutta Patil.
  • Modern Mathematics Studies: Algebra, Number Theory, Geometry, Calculus, problem-solving, and Statistics.
  • Technology Integration: Exposure to computers, Excel, programming languages, computational software, and mathematical typesetting tools.
